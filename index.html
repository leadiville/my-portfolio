Discover Me

Welcome to the world of a multifaceted professional blending the worlds of Web Development and Dance. Based in Lagos, Nigeria, I craft seamless digital experiences by day and express stories through movement by night. Explore my journey where technology meets artistry.

Web Development

I'm a passionate web developer who thrives on building impactful digital experiences. My journey began with a curiosity for how things work on the web, and over the years, I've honed my skills in both front- end and back-end development. I specialize in Node.js, PHP, and Laravel, and I'm equally comfortable working with JavaScript frameworks like React, Next.js, and Vue.js.

Dance/Entertainment

My dance journey began with a desire to be seen and to fulfill a purpose beyond the ordinary. Starting with hip-hop, I sought greater challenges and discovered breaking—a discipline that instilled in me the discipline and dedication necessary to excel. This path led me to become a leading B-Boy in West Africa, earning recognition through numerous competitions and performances. As an opinion leader for Red Bull Nigeria, I have further solidified my role as a key influencer and ambassador within the dance community.
